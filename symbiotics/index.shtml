Symbiotic stars discovered by IPHAS

This page contains the updated list of symbiotic stars discovered with the IPHAS survey. The table lists the name/coordinates of the sources, their r' magnitude, finding charts (size: 3 arcmin x 3 arcmin, North at the top and East to the left), spectra, and the reference to the discovery article. The spectrum of the source is available in FITS format and can be downloaded by clicking in the corresponding column. Spectra can be retrieved provided that the original source is properly referenced in any publication that results from their use. More details on each object, on the searching technique, and on the overall results of the project, can be found in the references.
