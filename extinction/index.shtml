A 3D extinction map of the Northern Galactic Plane based on IPHAS photometry

Warning

The map and catalogue linked on this page are likely to be the final product, to be made public when the accompanying paper is accepted, but minor changes remain possible.
Last update: 2014-05-20

Summary

In Sale et al (2014) we present a new 3D extinction map of the entire Northern Galactic Plane (|b| < 5°) based on an algorithm H-MEAD, previously described in Sale (2012), and IPHAS DR2 photometry.

There are two main products available for download: the 3D extinction map itself and a catalogue of stellar parameters for the ~38 million stars employed to build the map.
