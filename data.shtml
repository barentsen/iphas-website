IPHAS Data Access

IPHAS provides globally-calibrated catalogues and images, along with derived data products such as a 3D extinction map and lists of special objects. The primary data products are:

ProductWebsiteDescription
Catalogue iphas.org/dr2 Globally-calibrated source catalogue.
Images iphas.org/images Pipeline-reduced images.
Extinction map iphas.org/extinction A 3D extinction map derived from the catalogue data.

Acknowledging IPHAS

Publications arising from IPHAS data should cite and acknowledge the survey. The primary reference for defining the survey is Drew et al 2005 [bibtex]. Where the data have come from DR2, Barentsen et al 2014 [submitted] should be cited as well.

Here is text to be used in acknowledgment sections of papers:

All IPHAS data are processed by the Cambridge Astronomical Survey Unit, at the Institute of Astronomy in Cambridge. The bandmerged DR2 catalogue was assembled at the Centre for Astrophysics Research, University of Hertfordshire, supported by STFC grant ST/J001333/1.
