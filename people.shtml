PhD research

There is a project available to start in October 2015, at the University of Hertfordshire working with the IPHAS PI, Janet Drew, and with Geert Barentsen and Tim Gledhill as co-supervisors. A brief description of the project entitled "The outer Galaxy - a spectroscopic exploration of higher mass stars" can be found here (second entry). How/when to apply is described on this page. Funding is likely to be available for students of EU nationality.

Collaboration

If you are interested in exploiting IPHAS data and would like to talk to us about it -- either because you need some advice or pointers, or want to discuss a collaboration for some purpose -- you are most welcome to get in touch. We'd like to help. Our data pages are worth checking first. If you can't find what you are looking for there, contact the survey PI - details are given below.
